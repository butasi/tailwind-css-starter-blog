# A Guide to Eldoret, Kenya

Located in the Uasin Gishu County of Kenya, Eldoret is a bustling town with a population of around 500,000 people. It is known for its agricultural and technological industries, as well as its rich history and culture.

## Infrastructure

Eldoret is well-connected by road and has a modern transportation system. The town has several matatus (minibus taxis) and boda bodas (motorcycle taxis) that operate within and outside the town. There are also several bus companies that offer long-distance travel to other parts of the country.

In terms of healthcare, Eldoret has several hospitals and clinics that offer quality medical services. The Moi Teaching and Referral Hospital is the largest hospital in the town and serves as a referral center for the entire region.

Eldoret also has a good education system, with several primary, secondary, and tertiary institutions. The University of Eldoret is the main university in the town and offers courses in various fields such as engineering, education, and business.

## Places to Visit

Eldoret is home to several tourist attractions that are worth visiting. Here are a few places that you should consider adding to your itinerary:

### Kipkaren Museum

Located in Kipkaren, just outside Eldoret, the Kipkaren Museum is a popular tourist attraction. It is set on a farm that was owned by the late President Daniel Arap Moi and showcases the history and culture of the Kalenjin people.

### Cherangani Hills

The Cherangani Hills are located about 30 kilometers from Eldoret and offer a beautiful and peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of the town. The hills are home to several hiking and nature trails, as well as campsites where you can spend the night.

### Eldoret National Park

Located within the town, the Eldoret National Park is a small park that is home to several species of birds and animals. It is a great place for a leisurely walk or a picnic.

### Hotels and Accommodation

Eldoret has several hotels and guesthouses that cater to tourists. Some popular options include the Moi Gardens Hotel, the Mount Elgon Hotel, and the Silver Springs Hotel. There are also several budget-friendly options such as the Eldoret Guest House and the Kipkaren Guest House.

## Conclusion

Eldoret is a vibrant and welcoming town with a rich history and culture. From its modern infrastructure to its beautiful natural surroundings, there is something for everyone in Eldoret. Whether you are looking for a relaxing getaway or an adventure-filled vacation, Eldoret has something to offer.
